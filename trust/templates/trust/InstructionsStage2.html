Now we will explain the rules for making decisions in Part 2.

All participants remain in exactly the same role and pairing as in Part 1.

That means, if a participant was assigned to be Person A at the beginning of Part 1, he remains in this role in Part 2. The same is true for Person B.

For the second part you will be paired with exactly the same Person A or B you are going to be paired for the Part 1.

Both Person A and Person B can increase their payoffs in this Part 2 by providing an estimate of the paired person’s decision in Part 1.

The closer the provided estimate is to the actual decision of the paired participant in Part 1, the higher is the payoff in Part 2 a participant will receive.

As in Part 1, you have to make the estimation 12 times – one decision for a Person A or B from each of the 12 Cities.

You will be paid for only one of the 12 estimations.
