Person B - payoff calculation in Part 2
  • If Person B estimates Person A’s decision correctly, Person B’s payoff for Part 2 is 10 Tokens.
  • If Person B estimates Person A’s decision incorrectly, Person B’s payoff for Part 2 is 0 Tokens.
Example 1:

Suppose you are from City 6 in Region 6.

In Part 1, you decided to return 12 token in case Person A from City 2 transfers his endowment.

You are randomly paired with a participant A from City 2 in Region 2 who decided to transfer his endowment.

You estimated that Person A from City 2 will indeed transfer his endowment.

Your estimate is correct.

Your payoff in Part 2: 10

Example 2:

Suppose you are from City 1 in Region 1.

In Part 1, you decided to return 18 token in case Person A from City 8 transfers his endowment.

You are randomly paired with a participant A from City 8 in Region 8 who decided NOT to transfer his endowment.

You estimated that Person A from City 8 will transfer his initial endowment.

Your estimate is NOT correct.

Your payoff in Part 2: 0
