Payoff = Your endowment minus transfer plus return amount

Example 1:

Suppose you are from City 6 in Region 6.

You decided to transfer your endowment.

You are randomly paired with a participant B from City 2 in Region 2 who stated to return 15 Token in case you transferred your endowment.

Your payoff: 10 – 10 + 15= 15

equals Your endowment: 10

minus transfer: 10

plus return amount: 15

Example 2:

Suppose you are from City 8 in Region 8.

You decided not to transfer your endowment.

You are randomly paired with a participant B from City 1 in Region 1 who stated to return 9 Token in case you transferred your endowment.

Your payoff: 10 – 0 (+ 0) = 10

equals Your endowment: 10

minus transfer: (0)

plus return amount: (0)
