Estimation Task for Person B

Person B has to estimate whether or not the paired Person A transferred his initial endowment to her.

Since Person B at this moment does not know yet what city Person A is from, he will have to make his estimate for each of the 12 cities that are involved in this experiment.

Payoff calculation for Person B

Person B’s estimate whether or not his paired Person A will transfer his initial endowment to him is compared to the actual decision of Person A.

The estimate is converted into a payoff according to the following rule:

  • If Person B estimates Person A’s decision correctly, Person B’s payoff for Part 2 is 10 Tokens.
  • If Person B estimates Person A’s decision incorrectly, Person B’s payoff for Part 2 is 0 Tokens.
